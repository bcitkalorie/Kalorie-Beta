Fruit Salad

Ingridients:

  • 2 Kiwi
  • 1 Cup Watermelon, diced
  • 1 Cup Pineapple, diced
  • 1 Cup Strawberry, halfed
  • 1 Apple, diced
  • 1 Grapefruit - or Orange, sliced
  • 1 Cup Blueberry
  • Pinch of salt

Intructions:

  • Mix everything together in a large bowl
  • Add a pinch of salt will give your fruit salad a bold taste
  • You can let your fruit salad chill in the fridge gor 30 minutes for a extra punch of freshness
