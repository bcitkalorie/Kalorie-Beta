Overnight Oat

Ingridients:

  • 1/2 Cup Blueberry
  • 1 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Cup Rolled Oat
  • 2 Tbsp Honey (optional)

Intructions:

  • Mix the Rolled Oat, Yogurt (and Honey) together
  • Put your oat mixture in a mason jar
  • Put your oat jar in the fridge and leave overnight
  • Add in Blueberries and enjoy
