Almond Bar

Ingridients:

  • 1 Cup Almond
  • 1/2 Cup Honey - or Agave
  • 1/2 Cup sherred Coconut
  • 2 Cups rolled Oats
  • A pich of salt

Intructions:

  • Toast the Coconut and Almond until fragrant
  • Crush or slice the almonds to small pieces
  • Mix together the oat, Almonds, Coconuts and a pinch of salt in a large mixing bowl
  • Feeling extra? Add in chocolate chip, cinnamon or your topping of choice!
  • Add in the honey and quickly stir until combined
  • Press the mixture in to mold or roll it into small balls
